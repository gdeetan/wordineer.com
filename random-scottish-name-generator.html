    What Is a Random Scottish Name Generator?

    A random Scottish name generator creates Scottish-style given names, surnames, or full names from a curated list of real and historically associated name options. It is useful for writers, roleplayers, game developers, and name researchers who want names that feel Scottish without sorting through long reference lists by hand.

    How It Works

    Choose how many names you want, then narrow the list with filters like gender, style, meaning theme, starting letter, or popularity. The results update automatically whenever you change a filter, and the More options button keeps the mobile layout compact until you need the extra controls.

    Scottish Names: Modern, Gaelic, and Anglicized Forms

    Scottish names come from a mix of Scottish Gaelic, Scots, and anglicized forms. Some names keep a stronger Gaelic feel, while others are more familiar in modern English spelling. This generator balances both so you can lean toward authentic traditional forms or keep the results easier to read.

    Frequently asked questions

    Can I use these Scottish names in a story or game?
    Yes. You can use the generated names for fiction, games, screenplays, roleplaying campaigns, and creative projects. The meaning notes are short creative hints, not a formal naming authority.
    Are these real Scottish names?
    The generator uses a curated Scottish dataset with modern, Gaelic, and anglicized forms. Some results are more common than others, so the tool works best as a practical starting point for character naming and research.
    Why do some Scottish names look Gaelic and others look more familiar?
    Scottish naming includes Scottish Gaelic, Scots, and anglicized forms. Some names keep a stronger Highland or Gaelic flavor, while others are closer to the spelling most English readers expect.
    What does anglicized mean?
    Anglicized names are forms that have been adapted into English spelling or usage over time. They may still be Scottish in origin, but they usually look more familiar to readers who want a modern spelling.
    Is this random Scottish name generator free?
    Yes. It is free to use, with no account, no sign-up, and no server-side generation limit. The Scottish name data loads from a static JSON file so the page stays fast.
