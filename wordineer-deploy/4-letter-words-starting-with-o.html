4-letter words starting with O

O is one of the most common letters in English text — but it is a much less common word-starter, especially at four letters. Many O-words borrowed from Latin and French begin with prefixes like ob-, out-, or over- that produce words of five letters or more. The 28 four-letter O-words that do exist include some of the highest-frequency words in the language: open, over, once, only, oven. And despite the short list, it contains two of the best Scrabble plays in the alphabet at four letters — onyx and oryx, both scoring 14 points.

Common 4-letter words starting with O

These are the O-words that appear most frequently in everyday speech and writing — all carrying the Easy label and a practical starting point for any vocabulary or spelling exercise.

OathObeyOkayOmitOnceOnlyOpenOralOrcaOvalOvenOverOink

Several of these words appear among the 1,000 most used words in English. Once, only, open, and over are function words — they structure sentences and appear in nearly every paragraph of prose. Oven and oath are common nouns, but oral doubles as an adjective (spoken, as opposed to written) and a noun (a spoken examination). Obey is one of only a handful of four-letter O verbs in standard English.

4-letter O words for Scrabble and word games

O scores 1 point in Scrabble — and like other vowels, every point in an O word must come from the consonants around it. The O list contains two exceptional Scrabble words.

High-scoring 4-letter O words

Onyx and oryx both score 14 points (O=1, N/R=1, Y=4, X=8) — the same total, from a Y and an X. Onyx is the semi-precious black stone; oryx is the long-horned antelope. Both are short, real words, and both are valid in all major Scrabble rulesets. Ouzo scores 13 (O=1, U=1, Z=10, O=1) and is valid in SOWPODS (international). Obey scores 9 (O=1, B=3, E=1, Y=4). Oink scores 8 (O=1, I=1, N=1, K=5). Outside the X words, K is the best tile companion for O at four letters.

Useful O words for Scrabble board play

Onyx and oryx are genuinely strong plays whenever you draw X — both are short enough to fit most board positions and recognisable enough that opponents won't challenge them. Omen (O-M-E-N) is useful for placing M and N in a compact play. Opts handles a rack with O, P, T, S — all common tiles. Oust (to drive out) is four entirely common tiles in a legal play. Orzo (the pasta) is accepted in SOWPODS and useful for placing Z with low-value tiles.

4-letter O words for vocabulary building

Despite the smaller count, the O list spans promise and authority, animals, food, folklore, and music — and includes several words with fascinating histories.

Oaths, authority, and formal language

Oath, obey, and oyer all relate to authority, law, and formal declarations. An oath is a solemn promise — originally sworn on a sacred object or in the name of a deity, and still used in court testimony and sworn public office. Oyer is a legal term from Anglo-Norman French meaning "to hear" — an oyer court was a travelling royal court that heard serious criminal cases on circuit; the phrase oyez, oyez (hear ye) used by court criers shares the same root. Omit (to leave out) is widely used in formal and academic writing to indicate deliberate exclusion.

Animals

Orca, oryx, and oxen are all animals. The orca (killer whale) is actually the largest member of the dolphin family — its name comes from Latin orca (a type of whale). The oryx is a large desert antelope whose long straight horns, seen in profile, may be the origin of the unicorn legend — a side-view of the two-horned oryx can look like a single horn. Oxen (plural of ox) are castrated bulls that were among the first domesticated working animals; they powered agriculture across Eurasia for thousands of years before the horse collar made horse-drawn ploughs viable.

Food and drink

Oven, orzo, and ouzo all relate to food and drink. Orzo is an Italian pasta shaped like large grains of rice — its name means "barley" in Italian, reflecting its resemblance to the grain. It is used in soups, salads, and as a rice substitute. Ouzo is the national spirit of Greece, flavoured with anise and typically served with water which turns it milky white — a process called the "louche effect." Oven has been central to human cooking for at least 30,000 years, from clay pots heated in fire pits to modern electric ranges.

Folklore and the unusual

Ogre, omen, and oboe are perhaps the most character-rich words in the O list. An ogre is a man-eating giant from European folklore, popularised in modern culture via fairy tales and (less seriously) animated films — the word entered English from French, probably of Italian or Latin origin. An omen is any event interpreted as a sign of future fortune or misfortune — the Latin root omen meant literally "a sign" and gave us "ominous." The oboe is one of the oldest modern orchestral instruments, descended from the shawm of medieval Europe; its double-reed produces the distinctive penetrating tone used for concert A when orchestras tune.

Frequently asked questions

How many 4-letter words start with O?

This page includes 28 curated four-letter words starting with O. O is one of the least common word-starters at four letters in English — most common O-words are longer, and the vowel more typically appears mid-word.

What are good 4-letter words starting with O for Scrabble?

Onyx and oryx both score 14 points (Y=4, X=8) — the best O plays at four letters. Ouzo scores 13 (Z=10) in SOWPODS. Obey scores 9 (B=3, Y=4). Oink scores 8 (K=5). O itself is worth only 1 point — every point comes from X, Z, Y, B, or K.

Are these words valid for Scrabble?

Most standard words on this list are valid in Scrabble, but the official Scrabble word list (TWL for North America, SOWPODS for international play) is the authoritative source. Orzo and ouzo are accepted in SOWPODS. Oyer is a valid legal term in most rulesets. Verify before play.

What does the difficulty rating mean?

Easy words are common everyday vocabulary most adult speakers know well. Medium words are less frequent but widely understood. Hard words are uncommon, specialised, or archaic — useful for advanced vocabulary study or competitive Scrabble. Ratings reflect word frequency in standard English usage.

Why are there so few 4-letter words starting with O?

O is a vowel, and vowels are less common word-starters in English than consonants — especially at exactly four letters. Many O-words use prefixes like ob-, out-, or over- that produce longer words. The short four-letter O-words that do exist — open, over, once, only, oven — are among the most frequently used words in the English language.
