Affiliate Disclosure

We believe in being upfront about how we earn money to keep Wordineer free.

Short version: Some links on Wordineer are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we genuinely believe are useful to our users.

What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a standard and legal way for websites to earn revenue by referring visitors to third-party products or services. When you click an affiliate link on Wordineer and complete a qualifying action (such as subscribing to a paid plan), the merchant pays us a referral commission. The price you pay is never affected.

Our Grammarly Affiliate Partnership

Wordineer participates in the Grammarly Affiliate Program. Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that checks grammar, spelling, punctuation, style, and tone in real time. We recommend it because many of our users are writers, students, and professionals who can genuinely benefit from a dedicated writing assistant alongside our word tools.

Links to Grammarly on this site — including banners, in-article recommendations, and tool-page callouts — may be affiliate links. When you install Grammarly's free extension or upgrade to a paid plan through one of these links, Wordineer may receive a commission.

Our Honest Assessment of Grammarly

We recommend Grammarly because we believe it is a genuinely useful product, not because of the commission. Our candid take:

  • What it does well: Real-time grammar and spelling correction, tone detection, plagiarism checking (Premium), and browser/app integrations that work across almost every platform.
  • Who it's best for: Non-native English speakers, students, content creators, and professionals who write frequently and want a second set of eyes.
  • Limitations: The free plan is useful but limited. Advanced suggestions, clarity improvements, and plagiarism detection require a paid subscription. It is not a substitute for human editing on high-stakes documents.
  • Alternatives exist: ProWritingAid, Hemingway Editor, and LanguageTool are legitimate alternatives. We mention them elsewhere on the site because our goal is to help you, not just to earn commissions.

Our editorial opinions about Grammarly are our own and are not influenced by Grammarly or its affiliate terms.
