    Frequently asked questions

    What is a random word generator?
    A random word generator is a tool that selects words at random from a database of English words. You can filter results by word type, difficulty, length, and starting or ending letter. Lexigen's generator also shows definitions so you know exactly what each word means.
    What is the most common word in English?
    The most common written word in English is "the." The most frequently spoken word is "be" and its forms — is, are, was, were, am. These words are so common that they're excluded from most word generators in favour of more interesting vocabulary.
    Does every English word appear in this generator?
    No — and that's by design. We use a curated list of words that are genuinely useful and recognisable to most English speakers. Very obscure words are left out of the default list. If you want the full dictionary (500,000+ words), select "Extended" in the word type dropdown.
    What is the most random-sounding word?
    Purely subjective, but "aardvark" is a classic answer — it starts with a rare double-A and sounds unlike most English words. Other fan favourites include "hullabaloo," "kerfuffle," and "brouhaha." Hit generate a few times and you'll find your own.
    How do I use new words correctly in my writing?
    The best way to learn a new word is to use it in a sentence straight away. Write a sentence with the word, then check that it sounds natural in context. A tool like Grammarly can confirm your word choice is grammatically correct and tonally right before you publish or send.
