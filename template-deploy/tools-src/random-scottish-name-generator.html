    Can I use these Scottish names in a story or game?

    Are these real Scottish names?
    The generator uses a curated Scottish dataset with modern, Gaelic, and anglicized forms. Some results are more common than others, so the tool works best as a practical starting point for character naming and research.
    Why do some Scottish names look Gaelic and others look more familiar?
    Scottish naming includes Scottish Gaelic, Scots, and anglicized forms. Some names keep a stronger Highland or Gaelic flavor, while others are closer to the spelling most English readers expect.
    What does anglicized mean?
    Anglicized names are forms that have been adapted into English spelling or usage over time. They may still be Scottish in origin, but they usually look more familiar to readers who want a modern spelling.
