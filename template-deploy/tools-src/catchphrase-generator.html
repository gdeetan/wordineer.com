How to play Catchphrase

Players sit in a circle with two alternating teams. The player holding the device sees a word or phrase and gives verbal clues — no acting, no spelling, no saying part of the phrase — until their team guesses it correctly. Then they pass the device to the next player on the opposing team. When the timer runs out, the team currently holding the device loses a point. First team to 7 wins.

Tips for better games

  • Filter by age group: Use "Everyday" and "Food" for mixed ages, "Hard" for competitive groups.
  • Accelerating tension: The 60-second timer creates natural pressure. Try 30 seconds for more chaos.
  • Remote play: Share your screen and designate one person to tap "Next" when a word is guessed.

Frequently asked questions

How do you play Catchphrase?

Players alternate between two teams in a circle. The active player clues their team into the word without saying it, then passes the device when their team guesses. When the timer ends, the team holding the device loses that round.

Will words repeat during a session?

No. Words won't repeat until the full filtered pool is exhausted. Once every word has appeared, the pool resets automatically.

Can I filter words by category?

Yes — choose from Everyday, Actions, Places, People, Food, Entertainment, Idioms, or Hard. You can also filter by difficulty level to tailor the game to your group.

What does the timer do?

The timer counts down from 30, 60, or 90 seconds. A beep warns when 10 seconds remain, and a triple beep signals time's up. The team holding the device when the timer ends loses the round.
