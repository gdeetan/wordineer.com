    How the solver works

    Enter green letters in the position boxes, yellow letters in "Letters in word," and gray letters in "Not in word." Click Find Possibilities to see all matching words from the full Wordle guess dictionary (~12,000 valid words). This includes both likely answers and valid tactical guesses.

    The archive lists past Wordle answers only (the ~2,300 word answer pool). Today's answer is not shown — use the archive after you've played to check your result.

    Need help choosing a starting word? See our entropy-ranked list of best Wordle starting words — original analysis with a 10,000-game benchmark. Or use the Wordle Helper to get the optimal next guess for any active game.

    Frequently asked questions

    Does this include today's Wordle answer?
    No. The archive shows past answers only (yesterday and earlier). This lets you use the solver and look up puzzle history without accidentally seeing today's answer before you play.
    Why does the solver show words not in the archive?
    Wordle accepts about 12,000 valid English words as guesses, but only about 2,300 are in the answer pool. The solver searches all valid guesses — useful for tactical plays. Past Wordle answers all came from the smaller answer pool.
    What is the puzzle number?
    Wordle started on June 19, 2021 as Puzzle #1. Each day is a new consecutive number. The archive table shows the puzzle number for each past answer so you can find a specific game by date or number.
